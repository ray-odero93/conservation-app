What matters most to you in leadership, in your work, and in life?Â 

I have always believed that leadership is more about leading yourself first before you can lead others. I always ask myself, if I were someone else, would I follow my lead? What matters most to me is being able to first satisfy my own irreducible minimums of leadership before I can present myself to the rest of the world as a potential leader. Even in my work and life in general, I want to be able to have confidence in myself before I can give others the chance to have confidence in me. In summary, I want to lead by example, to be the change I want to see.
